About

Welcome to AYJ's student run Chess Club. Battle to victory with your peers in various time formats. Employ your favorite openings, tactics, and exploit your opponent's blunders!

We host tournaments every two weeks and provide many lessons as well as a community of great chess players and enthusiasts to help you further your game.

Join the community!

It is also highly encouraged that upon joining the club, you register an account on this site. We post announcements as well as dedicated lesson plans with each tournament.

The events tab shows our schedule for the year and there are lessons based on different skill levels.

Have some fun at AYJ's first Chess Club!

I have something in mind for a lesson!

Sure thing! We'd love to hear your idea, just shoot us a comment or suggestion at our email: ayjchess@gmail.com

Would you like to be an exec?

Fill out the exec application form here. Expect to get a reply within the next few days.

How do I know if I'm qualified enough?

The best thing to do is to try and apply. You might be just be qualified enough to be the president of this club!
